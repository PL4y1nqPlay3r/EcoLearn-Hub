About Us

EcoLearn Hub was founded in 2024 by Razaq Adeshina as part of a project in Web Design and Development course while also passionate about making sustainability knowledge accessible to everyone.

I believe that education is the key to creating a more sustainable future.

My goal is to empower individuals with knowledge and tools to lead more sustainable lives and contribute to global environmental conservation efforts.
